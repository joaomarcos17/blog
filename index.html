Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Ham and Cheese
  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Preparation

  1. Put the bread in the toast leave it until us toasted enough.
  2. Put the butter on the bread on one side.
  3. place the two slices of ham on over the bread with the butter side.
  4. Then place the cheese on top of it.
  5. Place the lettuce on top of it as well.
  6. slice the tomatoes than place two slices on top of the lettuce.
  7. Finally put the butter on the other bread slice and place on top of everything.
  8. Your Sandwich is done enjoy it!!
